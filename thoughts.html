Suits in the Millenial Workplace Who doesn't like looking good? Here, I talk about why the suit has fallen out of fashion with the latest generation.

This is one of the better pieces of writing that I've produced all year, in my opinion, which reflects the amount of work that I put into it. Part of the reason I think it turned out so well is that I'm incredibly interested in the topic, which is suits. Knowing that most people aren't as interested in suiting as I am, I decided to plan out the essay well in advance to make sure it wasn't too dry to get through. Furthermore, because I could read about the topic all day, doing research was kind of fun (for once!). To top it all off, I can genuinely say that I was excited to write this piece, something which I hope came through in my writing.
